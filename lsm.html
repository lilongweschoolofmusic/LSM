About LSM

A Professional Centre of Music Education & Performance

Lilongwe School of Music (LSM) is a professional music education and performance institution committed to the highest standards of musical training and artistic excellence. The school provides structured instruction in instrumental performance, vocal studies, and music theory, guided by internationally recognized frameworks.

LSM serves both as an academic institution and a performance hub, preparing students for graded music examinations while also offering professional music services for a wide range of events.

Through disciplined training and performance practice, the school develops musicians who are technically proficient, expressive, and performance-ready.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, structured music education that develops skilled and disciplined musicians while fostering creativity and excellence. We further strive to give a voice to the voiceless placing instruments in the hands of aspiring individuals and nurturing their talent through instrumental training, choir, and opera studies.

🌟 Our Vision

To be a leading centre of music education and performance in Malawi and beyond, recognized for producing well-rounded musicians and delivering outstanding musical experiences.

Examinations

Lilongwe School of Music (LSM) prepares candidates for internationally benchmarked music examinations in both Theory and Performance.

  • Music Theory: Initial to Grade 8
  • Instrumental Performance: Initial to Grade 8
  • Vocal Performance: Initial Grade to Grade 8

Diploma Qualifications:

  • Associate Diploma (ATCL)
  • Licentiate Diploma (LTCL)
  • Fellowship Diploma (FTCL)

Important Requirement: A pass in Grade 5 Music Theory is compulsory for candidates wishing to progress to Grades 6–8 Practical Examinations.
