About LSM

A Professional Centre of Music Education & Performance

"Where discipline meets artistry and talent is developed into excellence."

Lilongwe School of Music (LSM) is Malawi's leading conservatory-level institution, committed to the highest standards of musical training and artistic excellence. Guided by internationally recognized frameworks, the school provides structured instruction across instrumental performance, vocal studies, music theory, and ensemble training.

LSM serves both as an academic institution and a performance hub preparing students for graded examinations while offering professional music services for a diverse range of events. Whether you are beginning your musical journey or an advanced performer seeking diploma-level qualifications, LSM offers a structured pathway to excellence.

Apply Now Contact Us

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, structured music education that develops skilled and disciplined musicians while fostering creativity and excellence. We strive to give a voice to the voiceless placing instruments in the hands of aspiring individuals and nurturing their talent through instrumental training, choir, and opera studies.

✨ Our Vision

To be the leading centre of music education and performance in Malawi and beyond recognized for producing well-rounded musicians and delivering outstanding musical experiences.

🏛️ Our Conservatory Identity

LSM is modelled on conservatory best practice rigorous curriculum, ensemble training, performance development pathways, and internationally benchmarked examinations through Trinity College London, ABRSM, and the London College of Music.

Entry Requirements
  • ✔No prior experience required for beginners all absolute beginners are welcome across all age groups.
  • ✔Previous grade certificates (if applicable) for students transferring from other institutions.
  • ✔Instrument placement assessment. A brief informal assessment to determine the appropriate starting level.
  • ✔Grade 5 Theory pass is required for students wishing to proceed to Grades 6–8 Practical Examinations.
  • ✔Parental/guardian consent required for students under 18 years of age.
  • ✔Commitment to regular attendance and consistent practice is expected of all enrolled students.

The Malawi Youth Orchestra (MYO)

An exciting new initiative bringing together the finest young musicians and vocalists in Malawi for world-class orchestral and ensemble experiences.

About the Malawi Youth Orchestra

The Malawi Youth Orchestra (MYO) represents a groundbreaking initiative to develop and celebrate the next generation of musical performers in Malawi. Designed as an elite ensemble project under the Lilongwe School of Music, MYO will bring together the most talented instrumentalists and vocalists across the country to perform at the highest standards.

We are currently in the planning stages and are excited to share that we will be launching the Malawi Youth Orchestra in the future. This represents a major step in advancing music performance and education across our nation.

Planned Activities

  • ▸ Auditions & Recruitment: Nationwide auditions to identify and select the most promising young musicians across all instrumental and vocal disciplines.
  • ▸ Intensive Rehearsals: Regular ensemble rehearsals and coaching with master musicians to develop world-class performance standards.
  • ▸ Concert Performances: Public concerts and touring performances across Malawi, showcasing our nation's musical talent at the highest level.
  • ▸ International Collaborations: Partnerships with international orchestras and music institutions for collaborative performances and cultural exchange.
  • ▸ Music Education Workshops: Masterclasses and educational workshops delivered by leading musicians and conductors for MYO members and the broader student community.
  • ▸ Professional Development: Preparation for international competition opportunities and professional music careers.

Participation: The Malawi Youth Orchestra will feature the finest young instrumentalists and vocalists from across Malawi, selected through rigorous auditions. Whether you perform classical, contemporary, or traditional music, if you have the passion and dedication for excellence, there is a place for you.

Stay tuned for announcements regarding auditions, rehearsal schedules, and performance dates as we prepare for the launch of this transformative initiative in the near future.
