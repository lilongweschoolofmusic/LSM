About LSM

A Professional Centre of Music Education & Performance

"Where discipline meets artistry and talent is developed into excellence."

Lilongwe School of Music (LSM) is Malawi's leading conservatory-level institution, committed to the highest standards of musical training and artistic excellence. Guided by internationally recognized frameworks, the school provides structured instruction across instrumental performance, vocal studies, music theory, and ensemble training.

LSM serves both as an academic institution and a performance hub preparing students for graded examinations while offering professional music services for a diverse range of events. Whether you are beginning your musical journey or an advanced performer seeking diploma-level qualifications, LSM offers a structured pathway to excellence.

Apply Now Contact Us

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, structured music education that develops skilled and disciplined musicians while fostering creativity and excellence. We strive to give a voice to the voiceless placing instruments in the hands of aspiring individuals and nurturing their talent through instrumental training, choir, and opera studies.

✨ Our Vision

To be the leading centre of music education and performance in Malawi and beyond recognized for producing well-rounded musicians and delivering outstanding musical experiences.

🏛️ Our Conservatory Identity

LSM is modelled on conservatory best practice rigorous curriculum, ensemble training, performance development pathways, and internationally benchmarked examinations through Trinity College London, ABRSM, and the London College of Music.

Admissions

Join LSM Begin Your Musical Journey

LSM welcomes students of all ages and levels from beginners discovering music for the first time to advanced musicians pursuing diploma qualifications. Here is everything you need to know about joining.

Age Groups
5–8
Junior Beginners

Foundational music introduction aural training, basic instrument handling, and musical play.

9–13
Junior Students

Structured instrument lessons with early examination preparation from Initial Grade.

14–17
Senior Students

Advanced technique, ensemble training, and Grade 1–8 examination preparation.

18+
Adult Learners

All levels welcome. Flexible scheduling for working adults and diploma candidates.

Entry Requirements
  • ✔ No prior experience required for beginners all absolute beginners are welcome across all age groups.
  • ✔ Previous grade certificates (if applicable) for students transferring from other institutions or wishing to enter at a higher level.
  • ✔ Instrument placement assessment. A brief informal assessment to determine the appropriate starting level for new students.
  • ✔ Grade 5 Theory pass is required for students wishing to proceed to Grades 6–8 Practical Examinations.
  • ✔ Parental/guardian consent required for students under 18 years of age.
  • ✔ Commitment to regular attendance and consistent practice is expected of all enrolled students.
Application Process

How to Enrol

01

Submit Your Enquiry

Contact LSM via the enquiry form below, by phone, email, or WhatsApp. Indicate your instrument of interest, age group, and current level.

02

Placement Assessment

New students undergo a brief informal placement assessment to determine the appropriate curriculum level and examination path.

03

Confirm Enrolment

Upon confirmation of placement, complete enrolment by submitting registration details and any required documentation.

04

Begin Lessons

You will be assigned to a qualified instructor and integrated into the appropriate lesson schedule. Ensemble training begins from day one.
